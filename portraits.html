STUNNING PORTRAITS PHOTOGRAPHY

Unlike several other areas of photography, portraiture is personal. It is based on the interaction between the subject and photographer.
With portraiture, the idea is to use the likeness to communicate something about the people in the portraits, their personality, their essence. For this reason, it is necessary to have a relationship between a photographer and a subject. Dot Digitals specialise in capturing beautiful, striking images of all ages - bumps and babies in our Pregnancy and Newborn Shoots, and from toddlers to teenagers, to families in our Studio or Outdoor Shoots.
